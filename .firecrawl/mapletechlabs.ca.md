## Questions  Answered.

Everything you need to know before starting a project with us.

[Ask Us Anything](https://mapletechlabs.ca/#contact)

How long does a typical project take?

A focused MVP typically takes 8–12 weeks. A full-featured product is 16–24 weeks. Every project starts with a scoping session where we give you a precise timeline with fixed milestones.

How do you price your projects?

We work on a fixed-scope, fixed-price model. No hourly billing, no scope creep surprises. You know exactly what you're getting and what it costs before we write a single line of code.

Do you work with startups or only enterprises?

Both. We have a Rapid Launch programme specifically for early-stage startups (8 weeks, fixed price), and dedicated enterprise teams for complex multi-year engagements. We adapt to your stage.

Who owns the code and IP?

You do. 100%. Full source code, all intellectual property and credentials are transferred to you upon final payment. We sign an IP assignment agreement on day one.

Do you provide post-launch support?

Yes. We offer tiered SLAs from Essential (bug fixes + monitoring) to Scale (dedicated engineering team + 24/7 response). Most clients stay with us long after launch.

Can you work with our existing team?

Absolutely. We regularly augment internal teams, do code reviews, architect complex systems and fill specialist gaps. We can be a full outsourced partner or a specialist extension of your team.
